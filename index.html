Protherm (UK) Limited is a leading manufacturer and installer of ductwork and installer of HVAC insulation. Established in 1993 we are one of only a few companies that can offer both ductwork and insulation services. We are members of both BESA & TICA trade associations delivering services to the very highest quality. Our clients benefit from one less subcontractor to deal with as well as elevating co-ordination issues. Based in the Northeast of England we carry out works all across the UK with local tradesmen. We are used regularly by our clients because we offer very good value for money

BIM modeling & 3D cad drawings

Our own fully trained Cad team can work with all of the latest BIM modeling. Either turning your BIM models into a working cadduct drawing that can be exported to our cnc machines or devolop drawings from 2D drawings we can do the lot. No waiting for subcontract drawings to come back we can come to site with a laptop and resolve issues and have the parts manufactured in minutes.

Ductwork Fabrication

The fabrication shop is fitted out with the latest automated fabrication machinery with Firmac ADF Line, Esprit plasma machines,Spiro spiral forming machine, Gorelocker and seam welder for the production of circular fittings

Kingspan Kool Duct

An approved Kingspan Kool duct partner. The Kingspan KoolDuct System is an advanced and innovative pre-insulated rectangular HVAC ductwork system, which is installed in a single fix. The Kingspan KoolDuct System eliminates virtually all of the problems associated with galvanised sheet steel lagged with mineral fibre, whilst, at the same time, offering additional advantages to the specifying engineer, the architect, the M&E contractor, the fabricator, the facilities manager, the property developer and the building owner

Thermal Insulation

With over 20 Years as a member of TICA (Thermal insulation contractors association) We are able to provide insulation installation to the highest standards with our fully trained insulation engineers.
